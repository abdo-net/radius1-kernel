# 00-CONSTITUTION

## Purpose

This directory contains the supreme governance instrument of the Radius1 Kernel. The Constitution is the highest authority in the repository and establishes the fundamental principles, authority structures, and amendment procedures that govern all other documents.

## What Belongs Here

- `CONSTITUTION.md` — The supreme governance instrument
- Charters for governance bodies
- Inviolable structural and behavioral invariants
- Amendment records and constitutional history

## What Is Forbidden Here

- Implementation details or technology-specific content
- Product-specific requirements or features
- Operational procedures or workflows (belongs in `07-WORKFLOW/`)
- Standards or patterns (belong in `03-STANDARDS/` or `04-PATTERNS/`)
- Temporary or draft documents
- Anything that could be overridden by a lower-tier document

## Relation With the Rest of the Repository

- Overrides all other directories in case of conflict
- Empowers `02-GOVERNANCE/` bodies and defines their scope
- Establishes the authority framework that validates `01-META-MODEL/`
- All other documents derive their legitimacy from the Constitution
